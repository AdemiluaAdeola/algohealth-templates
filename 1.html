About AlgoHealth

AlgoHealth helps users understand their health better through AI-driven symptom analysis, smart health record management, and doctor connectivity — all in one seamless experience.

Key Features

AI Symptom Analysis

Input your symptoms or select from suggestions — the AI interprets and provides real-time insights.

Smart Health Records

Maintain an up-to-date digital health record securely for personalized AI recommendations.

Doctor Appointment Integration

Connect with verified doctors near you or share your AI summary for better consultations.

Symptom Tracking

The AI follows your progress and compiles reports automatically if symptoms persist.

How It Works

Step 1: Register

Create an account with basic info and health details.

Step 2: Input Health Data

Fill your health record for accurate AI-driven results.

Step 3: Chat with AI

Describe or select your symptoms; get instant health insights.

Step 4: Book Appointment

Choose to connect with a nearby doctor if needed — AI attaches your notes for smoother care.
