Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AlgoHealth

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

Our AI diagnostic system has been trained on millions of medical cases and achieves an accuracy rate of over 98% for common conditions. However, it's important to note that our AI is designed to assist, not replace, professional medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare provider for definitive diagnoses.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All health information is encrypted end-to-end and stored securely in compliance with HIPAA and GDPR regulations. We never share your personal health information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I use AlgoHealth for emergency situations?

No, AlgoHealth is not designed for emergency medical situations. If you're experiencing a medical emergency, please call your local emergency services immediately. Our platform is intended for non-emergency health concerns and routine healthcare management.

How much does AlgoHealth cost?

We offer a free basic plan that includes symptom analysis and health tracking. Our premium subscription ($9.99/month) provides additional features like priority doctor appointments, detailed health reports, and personalized wellness plans. We also offer family plans at discounted rates.

Can I connect AlgoHealth with my existing health apps?

Yes, AlgoHealth integrates with popular health and fitness apps like Apple Health, Google Fit, and Fitbit. This allows us to provide more comprehensive health insights by incorporating data from your wearable devices and other health applications.
